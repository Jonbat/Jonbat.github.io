This embedded systems project allows a user to take images from a phone and display it in real-time on a 32x32 LED display! Muscle Sensor LED Display

This embedded systems project detects muscle contractions and displays it on a 32x32 LED screen. The harder you contract your muscle, the larger the wave. Any large enough muscle is compatible, such as the biceps, forearms, or even forehead muscles!
